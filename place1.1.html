Newborn Monument

The **Newborn Monument** is one of Prishtina’s most iconic landmarks, symbolizing Kosovo's declaration of independence. It was unveiled on February 17, 2008, the day Kosovo declared independence from Serbia. The monument is constantly repainted every year to reflect the creativity and spirit of the people of Kosovo.

This monumental structure, with its ever-changing design, is not just a symbol of independence but also of the country's dynamic cultural scene. The monument serves as a living piece of art, with different messages and themes each year. It’s a must-visit for anyone exploring Prishtina’s rich history and cultural evolution.
