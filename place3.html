Vushtrri

Vushtrri is a town located in central Kosovo, known for its rich history and scenic beauty. Surrounded by beautiful hills and rivers, Vushtrri offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The town’s historical sites and tranquil surroundings make it a charming destination.

Places to Visit: The Castle of Vushtrri

The Castle of Vushtrri, dating back to the medieval period, offers visitors a glimpse into the region’s history. Situated on a hill, the castle provides panoramic views of the town and its surroundings. It's an ideal spot for history enthusiasts and those looking for a peaceful retreat in nature.
