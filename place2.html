Peja

Nestled at the foot of the Rugova Mountains, Peja is a natural paradise in western Kosovo. It’s known for its breathtaking landscapes, outdoor activities, and cultural landmarks. With Rugova Gorge and the Lumbardhi River nearby, Peja offers a perfect blend of adventure and tranquility.

Places to Visit: Patriarchate of Peja

The Patriarchate of Peja is a stunning Serbian Orthodox monastery dating back to the 13th century. This UNESCO World Heritage Site features vibrant frescoes, ancient architecture, and a serene setting surrounded by nature. It’s a spiritual and cultural hub that reflects the region’s diverse history.
