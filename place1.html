Prishtina

As the capital of Kosovo, Prishtina is a vibrant city filled with history, culture, and energy. Known for its lively atmosphere, Prishtina has a blend of modern architecture and historical landmarks. From bustling streets to tranquil parks, Prishtina is the heart of Kosovo.
